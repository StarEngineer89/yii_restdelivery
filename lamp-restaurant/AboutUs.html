WELCOME TO Lamp Restaurant

Here at ‘Lamp Restaurant’ we incorporate all these changes. Nowhere on earth has the art of vegetarian cuisine developed more than on the Indian Sub-Continent. Here in this restaurant we include a selection of vegetarian dishes. We offer traditional favourites and some traditional dishes which may be new to you. Try them as an accompaniment to meat or as a main course and you may have some delicious surprises! Of course we use only absolutely fresh, top quality ingredients blended with exotic, fragrant, pungent spices to produce the subtle flavours of Indian cuisine. No artificial food colourings or additives used. These dishes therefore may not have the vivid colours you associate with many dishes and use only the finest herbs and spices which give a truly authentic appearance and taste as would have been found in authentic Indian and Bangladeshi Cuisine.
